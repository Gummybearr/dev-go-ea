## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- depreciable asset인가
- basis가 얼마인가
- `placed in service`인가
- section 179 election 문제인가
- bonus depreciation 문제인가
- amortization / start-up / organizational cost 문제인가
- later recapture가 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- depreciation 문제는 `basis + placed in service`부터
- section 179와 bonus는 같은 것이 아니다
- `4562`는 depreciation cluster 레이더
- recapture는 disposition과 연결된다

## Day 3 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is most associated with the depreciation cluster?
   - A. Form 2553
   - B. Form 4562
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 1023

2. Which concept should you usually check early in a depreciation question?
   - A. Placed in service
   - B. Head of household
   - C. FBAR
   - D. E&P

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Section 179 and bonus depreciation are always the same thing
   - B. Basis is irrelevant to depreciation
   - C. Recapture can connect depreciation to disposition questions
   - D. Amortization is unrelated to business tax prep

4. Which cluster belongs together?
   - A. Section 179 / bonus / regular depreciation / amortization
   - B. HOH / EITC / FBAR
   - C. DNI / UBTI / 990
   - D. 1065 / 1120 / 1041 only

5. Which topic may be part of this cluster besides depreciation?
   - A. Start-up and organizational costs
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Filing status
   - D. Gift splitting

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Depreciation error correction can be a tested issue
   - B. Placed in service never matters
   - C. Recapture is unrelated to prior depreciation
   - D. Form 4562 is only for exempt organizations
