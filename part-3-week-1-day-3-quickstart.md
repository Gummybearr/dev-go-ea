### 문제

1. Which is a sanction type under Circular 230?
   - A. Censure
   - B. Standard deduction
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. QBI

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Incompetence/disreputable conduct questions can test specific conduct examples
   - B. Monetary sanctions are impossible
   - C. Sanctions never involve firms
   - D. Censure is a filing status

3. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. What conduct occurred, and what sanction framework applies?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this 1041?
   - D. Is this 4797?

4. Which statement is best?
   - A. Sanction structure and disreputable conduct should be read together
   - B. Censure and suspension mean exactly the same thing
   - C. Circular 230 sanctions never appear on Part 3
   - D. Conduct examples are irrelevant

5. Which is not part of the sanction-analysis mindset?
   - A. Identify the conduct
   - B. Identify possible sanction type
   - C. Ignore the penalty framework
   - D. Consider monetary vs nonmonetary consequences

6. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Part 3 sanctions questions are about procedural discipline, not tax calculations
   - B. They are mainly about COGS
   - C. They are mainly about basis
   - D. They are mainly about Schedule A
