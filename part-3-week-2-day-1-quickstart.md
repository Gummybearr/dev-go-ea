## Day 1 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is generally associated with representation authority?
   - A. Form 2848
   - B. Form 8821
   - C. Form 8948
   - D. Form 8453

2. Which form is generally associated with information authorization only?
   - A. Form 8821
   - B. Form 2848
   - C. Form 9465
   - D. Form 656

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Form 8821 generally does not by itself authorize representation
   - B. Form 8821 is stronger than Form 2848 for representation
   - C. CAF never matters
   - D. Future years are irrelevant

4. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Is this representation authority or information authorization?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this 4797?
   - D. Is this QBI?

5. Which issue may appear with 2848 questions?
   - A. CAF / future years
   - B. COGS
   - C. E&P
   - D. Like-kind exchange

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. 2848 vs 8821 is one of the highest-value trigger splits in Part 3
   - B. 2848 and 8821 can always be treated the same
   - C. Authorization forms are low value
   - D. CAF is unrelated
