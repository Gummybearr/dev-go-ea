## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- business asset인가
- basis가 얼마인가
- sale / exchange / disposition인가
- character가 무엇인가
- recapture가 붙는가
- converted property 또는 like-kind exchange인가
- 어느 폼으로 가는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- asset 문제는 `basis -> character -> form`
- business property sale이면 `4797`을 먼저 떠올린다
- recapture는 depreciation과 disposition을 연결한다
- converted property는 일반 business property처럼 단순 처리하면 위험하다

## Day 1 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form should often come to mind in a sale of business property question?
   - A. Form 2553
   - B. Form 4797
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 1023

2. In a business asset question, what should usually come first?
   - A. Basis
   - B. Filing status
   - C. FBAR
   - D. EITC

3. Which concept often connects prior depreciation to a later disposition?
   - A. Recapture
   - B. HOH
   - C. DNI
   - D. QBI

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Converted property can always be treated exactly like any ordinary business asset
   - B. Like-kind exchange is irrelevant to business assets
   - C. Character matters in a business asset disposition
   - D. Basis is optional

5. Which sequence is best?
   - A. Form first, basis last
   - B. Basis -> character -> form
   - C. Filing status -> dependent -> form
   - D. Credit -> deduction -> form

6. Which topic may appear as a special business-asset issue?
   - A. Like-kind exchange
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. FBAR only
   - D. Gift splitting
