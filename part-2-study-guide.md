## Part 2를 공부하는 방식

Part 2는 `세무 챕터 암기 시험`보다 `엔티티와 폼 흐름 시험`에 가깝다.

- 먼저 `어떤 entity인지`를 본다
- 그다음 `누구 레벨에서 과세되는지`를 본다
- 그다음 `basis / distribution / separately stated item / filing form`을 연결한다
- 계산보다 `character`와 `reporting path`를 먼저 잡는다

Part 2에서는 아래가 자동으로 떠야 한다.

- `sole prop -> Schedule C`
- `partnership -> Form 1065 / K-1`
- `C corp -> Form 1120`
- `S corp -> Form 1120S / K-1`
- `trust / estate -> Form 1041 / K-1`
- `exempt org -> Form 990 series`

### 오늘 잡아야 할 핵심 규칙

- `sole proprietorship`과 `single-member LLC default classification`은 자주 연결된다
- `partnership`은 보통 flow-through이다
- `C corporation`은 entity level taxation이 핵심이다
- `S corporation`은 flow-through이지만 자격요건과 basis 함정이 많다
- `LLC`는 법형태보다 tax classification을 따로 봐야 한다

## Day 1 미니퀴즈

답안은 `1B 2C ...` 형식으로 정리해서 스스로 채점하거나 튜터와 검토한다.

### 문제

1. Which entity is generally treated as a pass-through for federal income tax purposes?
   - A. C corporation
   - B. Partnership
   - C. Tax-exempt organization
   - D. Personal holding company

2. Which form is generally used for a partnership return?
   - A. Form 1120
   - B. Form 1120S
   - C. Form 1065
   - D. Schedule C

3. Which statement is correct regarding an LLC?
   - A. Every LLC is automatically taxed as an S corporation
   - B. Legal form and federal tax classification are always the same
   - C. LLC tax classification may depend on default rules or elections
   - D. An LLC can never be disregarded

4. Which entity is generally taxed at the corporate level?
   - A. Sole proprietorship
   - B. General partnership
   - C. S corporation
   - D. C corporation

5. Which form is commonly associated with an S corporation election?
   - A. Form 2553
   - B. Form 4797
   - C. Form 3115
   - D. Form 4562

6. If a question says `separately stated item` and `K-1`, which entity should immediately come to mind?
   - A. Wage employee
   - B. Pass-through entity
   - C. Exempt organization only
   - D. Household employer

7. Which issue is especially important in S corporation questions?
   - A. Shareholder basis
   - B. Filing status
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. FBAR threshold

8. Which return is generally used by a C corporation?
   - A. Form 1041
   - B. Form 1120
   - C. Form 1065
   - D. Form 990
