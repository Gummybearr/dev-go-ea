## Recommended Loop

한 module당 아래 루틴으로 공부한다.

1. `Preview`: 제목과 한 줄 결론만 읽는다.
2. `Lecture`: markdown 본문을 읽거나 m4a를 듣는다.
3. `Active recall`: 표를 덮고 판단 순서를 말로 설명한다.
4. `Question set`: 연결된 문제만 푼다.
5. `Error repair`: 틀린 문제는 답을 외우지 말고 trigger phrase를 적는다.

## Day 1 Core Sentences

1. `Form first, taxpayer second, rule third.`
2. `Deduction reduces taxable income; credit reduces tax.`
3. `Marital status is generally determined as of December 31.`
4. `HOH requires status, home cost, and qualifying person.`
5. `Dependent means QC first, QR second.`
6. `QC support asks whether the child provided more than half of the child's own support.`
7. `QR support asks whether the taxpayer provided more than half of the person's support.`
8. `Gross income is taxable first, exclusion second.`
9. `FBAR is filed through FinCEN BSA E-Filing.`
10. `Form 8938 does not replace FBAR.`
