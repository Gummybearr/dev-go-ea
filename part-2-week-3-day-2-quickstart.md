## Day 2 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. In a capitalization question, what should usually be decided first?
   - A. Deduct now or capitalize
   - B. Filing status
   - C. FBAR
   - D. Dependent status

2. Which pair should not be treated as identical?
   - A. Repair and improvement
   - B. Asset and basis
   - C. Form 4797 and disposition
   - D. Accounting method and Form 3115

3. Which concept can affect whether an expenditure is capitalized?
   - A. Unit of property
   - B. HOH
   - C. DNI
   - D. EITC

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Elections can matter in capitalization questions
   - B. Repair regulations never involve judgment
   - C. Improvement is always immediately deductible
   - D. Capitalization has nothing to do with business assets

5. Which is a classic capitalization signal?
   - A. Betterment / restoration / adaptation
   - B. Filing status / dependency
   - C. FBAR / 8938
   - D. AOTC / LLC

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Repair and maintenance issues are part of Part 2 business tax prep
   - B. Capitalization questions belong only to Part 1 individuals
   - C. Unit of property never matters
   - D. Elections are irrelevant
