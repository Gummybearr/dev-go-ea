## Day 2 핵심

- business expense는 먼저 `ordinary / necessary / reasonable / business purpose`
- expense cluster:
  - compensation
  - rent
  - meals / travel / gifts
  - vehicle
  - insurance
  - taxes
  - home office

## 오늘 목표

- 자주 나오는 business expenses를 한 번에 정리한다
- compensation, meals, travel, vehicle, insurance, taxes를 구분한다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- business purpose가 있는가
- ordinary / necessary / reasonable인가
- currently deductible인가
- travel / meals / gifts / vehicle처럼 별도 제한이 있는가
- payroll or employment tax 이슈가 같이 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- expense 문제는 먼저 `business purpose`
- meals와 travel은 같은 주제가 아니다
- vehicle 문제는 records와 같이 본다
- taxes / penalties / assessments는 같은 취급이 아닐 수 있다
- home office는 따로 의식한다

## Day 2 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. In a business expense question, what should usually be checked first?
   - A. Business purpose
   - B. Filing status
   - C. FBAR
   - D. Child tax credit

2. Which pair should not be treated as automatically identical?
   - A. Meals and travel
   - B. Schedule C and business activity
   - C. Compensation and wages
   - D. Rent and business use

3. Vehicle expense questions often require attention to:
   - A. Records
   - B. HOH
   - C. K-1 only
   - D. E&P

4. Which issue may overlap with business expense questions?
   - A. Employment tax
   - B. FBAR only
   - C. Gift tax only
   - D. Estate tax only

5. Home office is best treated as:
   - A. A standalone recurring business-expense topic
   - B. A corporation-only issue
   - C. An exempt-organization issue
   - D. A gift-tax issue

6. Which statement is generally best?
   - A. Penalties and deductible business taxes are always treated the same
   - B. Meals, travel, gifts, and vehicle issues can carry separate rules
   - C. Records never matter in business expense questions
   - D. Reasonableness is irrelevant

### 정답

`1A 2A 3A 4A 5A 6B`
