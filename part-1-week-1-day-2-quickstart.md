## 오늘 목표

- filing status를 `status -> support -> qualifying person` 순서로 본다
- `HOH`와 `QSS`를 다른 트랙으로 인식한다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- year-end marital status가 무엇인가
- married, unmarried, considered unmarried 중 무엇인가
- home support test가 충족되는가
- qualifying person이 있는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- marital status is usually determined as of `December 31`
- `HOH` is not just `has a child`
- `MFS` often carries deduction and credit limits

## Day 2 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Filing status is usually determined based on which date?
   - A. January 1
   - B. April 15
   - C. December 31
   - D. Date of first paycheck

2. Which is a core HOH requirement?
   - A. Filing jointly
   - B. Paying more than half the cost of keeping up the home
   - C. Owning foreign accounts
   - D. Having wage income only

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. MFS and MFJ are usually treated identically
   - B. MFS often limits credits or deductions
   - C. QSS is the same as Single
   - D. HOH applies to all unmarried taxpayers

4. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. What was the taxpayer's marital status at year-end?
   - B. Is this Form 4797?
   - C. Is this basis or FMV?
   - D. Is this a K-1?

5. Which statement is best?
   - A. HOH turns only on whether a child exists
   - B. HOH requires both home-support and qualifying-person analysis
   - C. HOH is always better than MFJ
   - D. QSS is unrelated to spouse status

6. Which filing status is associated with surviving spouse treatment?
   - A. QSS
   - B. MFS
   - C. Single
   - D. HOH only
