## 오늘 핵심 원칙

Part 1 Day 1에서 가장 중요한 문장은 이것이다.

`Form first, taxpayer second, rule third.`

문제를 보면 먼저 아래를 순서대로 본다.

- 누가 신고하는가
- filing status가 무엇인가
- dependent가 걸리는가
- taxable인가 excluded인가
- 어느 줄 / 어느 스케줄 / 어느 폼으로 가는가

## 오늘 꼭 잡을 것

- `Gross income -> Adjustments -> AGI -> Deduction -> Taxable income -> Tax -> Credits -> Other taxes -> Payments`
- `marital status is usually determined as of December 31`
- `HOH`는 status, support of home, qualifying person을 같이 본다
- dependent 문제는 먼저 `QC vs QR`로 가른다
- 초반 문제는 숫자보다 `who / when / form`이 더 중요하다

## 한 장으로 외울 흐름

| Step | 질문 |
|---|---|
| Gross income | 이 항목이 원칙적으로 소득인가 |
| Adjustments | AGI 전에 빠지는가 |
| Deduction | standard인가 itemized인가 |
| Tax | regular tax 외에 other tax가 붙는가 |
| Credits | 누가 claim하는가 |
| Reporting | 추가 폼이 필요한가 |

## 오늘의 트리거 문장

- `married late in year` -> 보통 married status부터
- `child or parent in household` -> HOH 요건 먼저
- `dependent question` -> QC or QR first
- `foreign account` -> later check FBAR vs 8938

## Day 1 미니퀴즈

답안은 `1B 2B ...` 형식으로 정리해서 스스로 채점하거나 튜터와 검토한다.

### 문제

1. A taxpayer married on `November 30, 2025`. For filing-status purposes, which is correct?
   - A. Single for most of the year
   - B. Married for the whole year
   - C. Must file HOH
   - D. Cannot file jointly

2. Which is a required test for a `qualifying child`?
   - A. Disability test
   - B. Residency test
   - C. Fixed gross-income threshold test
   - D. Must be age 24 or older

3. In the basic `Form 1040` flow, what comes right after `AGI`?
   - A. Other taxes
   - B. Standard or itemized deduction to arrive at taxable income
   - C. Withholding
   - D. Refundable credits

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. HOH only requires a child
   - B. HOH requires status, support of home, and a qualifying person
   - C. HOH is available to every married taxpayer
   - D. HOH is the same as MFJ

5. Which item is generally excluded from gross income?
   - A. Property received for legal services
   - B. Illegal income
   - C. Gift from a parent
   - D. Gambling winnings

6. Which statement is correct?
   - A. FBAR is filed with Form 1040
   - B. Form 8938 replaces FBAR
   - C. FBAR is filed online with FinCEN
   - D. FBAR has no penalty exposure

`1B 2B 3B 4B 5C 6C`
