## Day 5 핵심

- `S corp`는 pass-through
- return은 `Form 1120S`
- election은 `Form 2553`
- `K-1`, separately stated items, shareholder basis가 핵심
- qualification, distributions, losses in excess of basis가 고빈도

## 오늘 목표

- S corp qualification, election, basis, distributions를 정리한다
- `1120S -> K-1 -> shareholder return` 흐름을 잡는다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- S corp인가
- 자격요건을 충족하는가
- election issue인가
- K-1 / separately stated item인가
- shareholder basis 또는 loan basis가 중요한가
- distribution 또는 loss limitation 문제인가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- `1120S -> K-1 -> shareholder`
- `2553 -> election`
- S corp loss question -> `shareholder basis first`
- qualification rules는 자주 기본기로 나온다

### 문제

1. Which form is commonly associated with an S corporation election?
   - A. Form 2553
   - B. Form 4562
   - C. Form 4797
   - D. Form 3115

2. Which return is generally used by an S corporation?
   - A. Form 1120
   - B. Form 1120S
   - C. Form 1065
   - D. Form 1041

3. Which issue is especially central in many S corporation loss questions?
   - A. Filing status
   - B. Shareholder basis
   - C. Standard deduction
   - D. FBAR

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. S corporations are always taxed like C corporations
   - B. S corporation items may flow through on K-1
   - C. S elections are made on Form 4797
   - D. Qualification rules are not tested

5. Which concept may matter in S corporation basis questions?
   - A. Loan basis
   - B. Head of household
   - C. Child care credit
   - D. Schedule A

6. In an S corporation question involving distributions or losses, what should often come first?
   - A. Shareholder basis
   - B. EITC
   - C. FBAR threshold
   - D. Medical deductions
