이 파일은 Part 2 Week 1 `Day 4 corporation formation / section 351`만 바로 공부할 수 있게 압축한 노트다.

## Day 4 핵심

- formation 문제는 먼저 `351` 가능성을 본다
- `property`, `services`, `control`, `boot`, `liabilities`를 같이 본다
- services for stock은 property contribution과 다르게 본다
- liabilities assumed는 결과를 바꿀 수 있다

## 오늘 목표

- corporate formation 문제에서 `section 351`을 인식한다
- services for stock, boot, liability, control을 구분한다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- corporation formation인가
- transfer한 것이 property인가 services인가
- transferor group이 control을 가지는가
- stock만 받는가, `boot`가 붙는가
- liabilities assumed가 있는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- `351`은 transfer to corporation + control 문맥
- services for stock은 property transfer와 같은 취급이 아니다
- boot가 붙으면 경고등
- liabilities assumed는 basis/result 함정이 된다

## Day 4 미니퀴즈

답안은 `1B 2C ...` 형식으로 정리해서 스스로 채점하거나 튜터와 검토한다.

### 문제

1. In a corporation formation problem, which topic should often come to mind first?
   - A. Schedule A
   - B. Section 351
   - C. FBAR
   - D. Child tax credit

2. Which issue must often be tested in a section 351 question?
   - A. Filing status
   - B. Standard deduction
   - C. Control
   - D. Head of household

3. Which statement is correct?
   - A. Services for stock are always treated the same as property transfer
   - B. Boot is irrelevant in formation questions
   - C. Liabilities assumed can affect the tax result
   - D. Control never matters

4. Which item is a classic warning sign in a formation question?
   - A. Boot
   - B. Schedule SE
   - C. K-1 only
   - D. Form 990

5. In a 351-style question, what should you distinguish early?
   - A. Property vs services
   - B. MFJ vs MFS
   - C. AOTC vs LLC
   - D. FBAR vs 8938

6. Which statement is generally best?
   - A. Formation questions never involve basis concerns
   - B. Liabilities can be ignored in contribution questions
   - C. Control, boot, and liabilities are recurring formation issues
   - D. Section 351 applies only to S elections
