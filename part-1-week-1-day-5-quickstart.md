## Day 5 핵심

- gross income
- exclusions

- scholarship
- life insurance

## 오늘 목표

- income 문제를 `included / excluded / depends`로 빠르게 나눈다
- service compensation과 gift를 섞지 않는다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- 특별 제외 규정이 있는가
- services 대가인가
- gift / inheritance / scholarship facts가 있는가
- taxability가 complete exclusion인지 partial인지 depends인지

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- if not excluded, start by treating it as gross income
- property received for services is generally income
- gifts and inheritances may be excluded, but income from them is separate

## 오늘 할 일

- gross income / exclusion 문제 `30~40문제`

## Day 5 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which item is generally included in gross income?
   - A. Artwork received for legal services
   - B. Gift from a parent
   - C. Basic life insurance proceeds paid by reason of death
   - D. Inheritance itself

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Gifts are always taxable wages
   - B. Property received for services is generally income
   - C. Illegal income is excluded
   - D. Gambling winnings are excluded

3. Which category often belongs in `Depends`?
   - A. Scholarship
   - B. Wages
   - C. Illegal income
   - D. Fee for services

4. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Is there a specific exclusion rule?
   - B. Is this Form 2553?
   - C. Is this K-1 basis?
   - D. Is this Appeals timing?

5. Which statement is best?
   - A. Gift income and income produced by gifted property are the same
   - B. The gift itself and income later produced from it are different tax questions
   - C. Gross income questions ignore source
   - D. Scholarships are always fully taxable

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Day 5 is mostly an inclusion-exclusion classification drill
   - B. Day 5 is about estate tax returns
   - C. Day 5 is about collections
   - D. Day 5 is about M-1 reconciliation
