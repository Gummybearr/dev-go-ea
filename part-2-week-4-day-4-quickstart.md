## 오늘 목표

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- farmer topic인가 rental topic인가
- farm rental인가 일반 rental인가
- `Form 4835`가 필요한가
- Schedule J / SE / estimated tax 이슈가 붙는가
- real estate professional / passive loss limitation이 붙는가
- deposit / prepaid rent / mixed-use allocation 문제인가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- farm rental과 일반 rental을 섞지 않는다
- rental 문제는 거의 항상 passive loss limitation을 의식한다
- prepaid rent와 deposit은 같지 않다
- mixed-use / vacation home는 allocation 함정이 많다

## Day 4 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is a strong signal for farm rental issues?
   - A. Form 4835
   - B. Form 2553
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 1023

2. Which statement is generally correct about rental-property questions?
   - A. Passive loss limitation is often a key issue
   - B. Deposits and prepaid rent are always identical
   - C. Real estate professional status never matters
   - D. Mixed-use allocation is irrelevant

3. Which pair should be kept separate?
   - A. Farm rental and general rental
   - B. SEP and SIMPLE
   - C. 4797 and 3115
   - D. 1023 and 990

4. Which issue is a classic rental warning signal?
   - A. Real estate professional
   - B. HOH
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. E&P

5. Which question should you ask early in a rental problem?
   - A. Is there a passive loss limitation issue?
   - B. Is this MFJ?
   - C. Is this DNI?
   - D. Is this section 351?

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Prepaid rent and deposits should be distinguished
   - B. Mixed-use property never needs allocation
   - C. Form 4835 is an exempt-org form
