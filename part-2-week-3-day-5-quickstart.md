## Day 5 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. In a business advising question, what may matter beyond pure tax rate comparison?
   - A. Filing and compliance obligations
   - B. HOH only
   - C. FBAR only
   - D. Estate tax only

2. Which issue is a classic advising warning sign?
   - A. Commingling personal and business accounts
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Standard deduction
   - D. Gift splitting

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Worker classification can recur in advising questions
   - B. Entity selection is always solved by picking the lowest rate
   - C. Records are irrelevant in advising
   - D. Timing of income and expenses never matters

4. Which cluster belongs together?
   - A. Filing obligations / payment obligations / records
   - B. HOH / MFJ / EITC
   - C. FBAR / 8938 / 709
   - D. DNI / corpus / 1041

5. Which statement is best?
   - A. Advising problems may be about obligations, not just math
   - B. Advising problems are always pure computation
   - C. Commingling is never tested
   - D. Worker classification only belongs in payroll chapters

6. Which issue may appear as planning?
   - A. Timing of income and expenses
   - B. Filing status for dependents
   - C. Education credits
   - D. FBAR only
