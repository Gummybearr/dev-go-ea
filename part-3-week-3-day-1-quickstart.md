## 오늘 목표

- collection 문제를 `stage -> remedy -> form -> deadline` 순서로 본다
- `installment agreement`, `OIC`, `CDP`를 섞지 않게 만든다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- 지금 단계가 billing / notice / collection / appeal 중 어디인가
- 바로 떠오르는 remedy가 무엇인가
- `12153`, `9465`, `656` 중 어떤 문서가 중심인가
- levy suspension or review rights가 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- `Form 9465` is generally for an installment agreement request
- `Form 656` is generally for an offer in compromise
- `Form 12153` is generally for a CDP or equivalent hearing request
- collection 문제는 거의 항상 `time window`와 `procedural stage`가 중요하다

1. Which form is generally used to request a monthly installment agreement?
   - A. Form 9465
   - B. Form 656
   - C. Form 843
   - D. Form 1040-X

2. Which form is generally associated with an offer in compromise?
   - A. Form 12153
   - B. Form 9465
   - C. Form 656
   - D. Form 8879

3. Which document is generally tied to a CDP or equivalent hearing request?
   - A. Form 12153
   - B. Form 2848
   - C. Form 8948
   - D. Form 8453

4. Which split is foundational in collection questions?
   - A. lien vs levy
   - B. AGI vs taxable income
   - C. basis vs holding period
   - D. 1120 vs 1120S

5. Which statement is generally best?
   - A. Collection remedies should be matched to the procedural stage
   - B. All collection forms can be treated the same
   - C. Deadlines rarely matter
   - D. Appeals rights are unrelated

6. Which issue can appear in collection questions?

   - B. kiddie tax
