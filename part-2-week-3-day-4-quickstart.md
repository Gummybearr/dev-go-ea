## Day 4 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. In a books-to-tax question, what should you usually ask first?
   - A. Why is there a difference?
   - B. Which filing status?
   - C. Which education credit?
   - D. Which FBAR threshold?

2. Which concept pair belongs together here?
   - A. Income statement and balance sheet
   - B. HOH and EITC
   - C. FBAR and 8938
   - D. AOTC and LLC

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. M-1 / M-2 / M-3 are not just blind computation topics
   - B. Related party activity is never important
   - C. Owner loans can never be confused with distributions
   - D. K-1 items are unrelated to financial analysis

4. Which issue can overlap with books-to-tax analysis?
   - A. Pass-through activity
   - B. HOH
   - C. Child credit
   - D. FBAR

5. Which is a danger area in this domain?
   - A. Loans to and from owners
   - B. Form 709
   - C. Form 8379
   - D. Form 8863

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Related party activity should trigger extra caution
   - B. Books vs tax never matters in Part 2
   - C. M schedules have nothing to do with reconciliation
   - D. Owner loans are always routine and harmless
