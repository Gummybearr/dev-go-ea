- `Schedule K-1` -> pass-through items and item character

## Day 6 체크리스트

- `4797`와 `3115`를 섞지 않는가
- K-1와 books-to-tax adjustments를 함께 읽을 수 있는가
- capitalization / repair / recapture를 연결하는가
- advising 문제에서도 reporting path를 떠올리는가

## Day 6 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is most associated with sale of business property?
   - A. Form 4797
   - B. Form 3115
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 2553

2. Which form is most associated with accounting method change?
   - A. Form 4797
   - B. Form 3115
   - C. Form 709
   - D. Form 8379

3. Which document is a strong pass-through signal?
   - A. Schedule K-1
   - B. Form 8863
   - C. Form 709
   - D. Form 8938

4. Which schedules are strong books-to-tax reconciliation signals?
   - A. M-1 / M-2 / M-3
   - B. Schedule A / B only
   - C. Form 2441 / 8863
   - D. 709 / 706

5. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. 4797 and 3115 should not be mixed up
   - B. K-1 is irrelevant to pass-through items
   - C. M schedules never matter
   - D. Advising has no reporting-path component

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Form numbers should trigger topic recognition
   - B. Form numbers are not useful in Part 2
   - C. Reporting path does not matter
   - D. K-1 belongs only to Part 1 individuals
