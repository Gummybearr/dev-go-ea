## Day 3 핵심

- `C corporation`은 보통 entity-level tax
- return은 `Form 1120`
- distribution 문제는 `E&P`가 핵심
- dividend, return of capital, redemption, liquidation은 다 같은 것이 아니다
- estimated tax, due date, penalties, accumulated earnings tax가 자주 붙는다

## 오늘 목표

- C corporation의 큰 흐름을 잡는다
- `Form 1120` 감각을 만든다
- `E&P`, distributions, redemptions, liquidations를 구분한다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- C corp인가
- entity level tax인가
- distribution인가
- `E&P`를 봐야 하는가
- dividend / redemption / liquidation 중 무엇인가
- due date / estimated tax / penalty 이슈가 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- `1120 -> entity-level tax`
- distribution 문제면 `E&P first`
- redemption과 liquidation은 같은 이벤트가 아니다
- accumulated earnings tax는 planning trap이다

## Day 3 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. A C corporation is generally taxed:
   - A. Only at the shareholder level
   - B. At the entity level
   - C. As a disregarded entity
   - D. Only through K-1 items

2. Which return is generally filed by a C corporation?
   - A. Form 1065
   - B. Form 1120S
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 1120

3. Which concept is especially important in many C corporation distribution questions?
   - A. Filing status
   - B. E&P
   - C. Standard deduction
   - D. FBAR

4. Which statement is correct?
   - A. Liquidation and redemption are always the same
   - B. C corporations issue K-1s to shareholders
   - C. E&P is irrelevant to corporate distributions
   - D. Estimated tax and penalties can be tested in C corporation questions

5. Which topic is a classic C corporation planning trap?
   - A. Child tax credit
   - B. Accumulated earnings tax
   - C. Head of household
   - D. FBAR filing

6. In a C corporation distribution problem, what should you usually think first?
   - A. E&P
   - B. Schedule A
   - C. K-1
   - D. Self-employment income

`1B 2D 3B 4D 5B 6A`
