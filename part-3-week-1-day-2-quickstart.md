## Day 2 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which issue belongs in the practice-authority cluster?
   - A. PTIN and suitability
   - B. COGS
   - C. Basis
   - D. QBI

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Unenrolled preparers generally have the same broad authority as EAs
   - B. Practice authority questions can involve designation wording
   - C. PTIN is irrelevant
   - D. CE never matters

3. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Who may practice here, and to what extent?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this section 351?
   - D. Is this 4797?

4. Which statement is best?
   - A. Scope of practice and preparer requirements should be kept distinct
   - B. PTIN and CE are never tested
   - C. Designation language cannot be tested
   - D. All practitioners have identical rights

5. Which issue is a requirement-type issue rather than a collection remedy?
   - A. Continuing education
   - B. Offer in compromise
   - C. CDP hearing
   - D. Levy release

6. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Practice-authority questions often ask whether an EA may perform a task
   - B. Practice-authority questions are only about QBI
   - C. PTIN belongs only in Part 2
   - D. CE is unrelated to EAs
