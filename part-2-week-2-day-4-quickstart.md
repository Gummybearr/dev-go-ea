## Day 4 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. QBI is always identical to gross business receipts
   - B. QBI is always identical to any business deduction
   - C. QBI is not simply the same concept as all business income
   - D. QBI only matters for trusts

2. Which term should act as a warning signal in a QBI problem?
   - A. SSTB
   - B. HOH
   - C. FBAR
   - D. DNI

3. Which concept may appear in QBI limitation discussions?
   - A. UBIA
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Schedule A
   - D. Estate tax

4. Which statement is best about business credits?
   - A. Eligibility and carry structure may matter
   - B. They are identical to ordinary deductions
   - C. They never require separate analysis
   - D. They only apply to sole proprietors

5. NOL is best viewed as:
   - A. Just another routine current-year expense item
   - B. A structure with carry logic
   - C. The same thing as QBI
   - D. A filing-status concept

6. In a QBI problem, what should you separate early?
   - A. QBI vs ordinary business income concepts
   - B. FBAR vs 8938
   - C. HOH vs MFJ
   - D. AOTC vs LLC
