## 최소 비교표 예시

| Entity | Taxed Where | Return | K-1 | Basis Issue | Biggest Trap |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sole prop | Owner | `Schedule C` | No | Usually not central like pass-through basis | hobby vs business |
| Partnership | Pass-through | `1065` | Yes | Partner basis | guaranteed payment / liabilities |
| C corp | Entity | `1120` | No | E&P more central than owner basis | distributions / E&P |
| S corp | Pass-through | `1120S` | Yes | Shareholder basis / loan basis | qualification / basis limits |

## Day 6 미니퀴즈

답안은 `1C 2B ...` 형식으로 정리해서 스스로 채점하거나 튜터와 검토한다.

### 문제

1. Which entity generally files Form 1065?
   - A. C corporation
   - B. S corporation
   - C. Partnership
   - D. Exempt organization

2. Which entity generally files Form 1120S?
   - A. Partnership
   - B. S corporation
   - C. Sole proprietorship
   - D. Estate

3. Which pair is correctly matched?
   - A. C corporation -> K-1
   - B. Partnership -> Form 1120
   - C. Sole proprietorship -> Schedule C
   - D. S corporation -> Form 1041

4. Which issue is especially central in many partnership questions?
   - A. Partner basis
   - B. HOH status
   - C. AOTC
   - D. FBAR

5. Which issue is especially central in many S corporation questions?
   - A. Shareholder basis
   - B. Schedule A
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. Head of household

6. Which pair should not be treated as the same topic?
   - A. Form 2553 and S election
   - B. Section 351 and corporate formation
   - C. Partnership basis and shareholder basis
   - D. Form 1120 and C corporation
