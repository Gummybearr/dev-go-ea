## Day 6 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is commonly associated with wages?
   - A. W-2
   - B. 1098-T
   - C. 1099-DIV
   - D. 1099-INT

2. Which form is commonly associated with qualified tuition statement reporting?
   - A. 1098-T
   - B. W-2
   - C. 1099-NEC
   - D. 1099-B

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. A `1098-T` guarantees an education credit
   - B. A `1098-T` is only part of the education-credit analysis
   - C. `1099-NEC` means wage withholding
   - D. Form numbers are low value

4. Which form should make you think of self-employment issues?
   - A. 1099-NEC
   - B. 1099-INT
   - C. 1098-T
   - D. W-2

5. Which question should you ask in an education-credit problem?
   - A. Who claims the student?
   - B. Is this a collection appeal?
   - C. Is this a gift-splitting issue?
   - D. Is this an S election?

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Week 1 Day 6 is about turning form numbers into tax-topic recognition
   - B. Week 1 Day 6 is mostly about depreciation
   - C. Week 1 Day 6 is mostly about estate tax
   - D. Week 1 Day 6 is mostly about Circular 230
