## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- accounting method 문제인가
- cash / accrual / hybrid 중 무엇인가
- method change인가
- improper method correction인가
- accounting period와 혼동하고 있지 않은가
- `3115` 신호어가 있는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- accounting method와 accounting period는 다르다
- method change면 `3115` 가능성을 떠올린다
- cash vs accrual은 income recognition timing과 연결된다

## Day 3 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is most associated with a change in accounting method?
   - A. Form 3115
   - B. Form 2553
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 709

2. Which distinction matters here?
   - A. Accounting method vs accounting period
   - B. HOH vs MFJ
   - C. FBAR vs 8938
   - D. AOTC vs LLC

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Cash and accrual affect timing of income recognition
   - B. Form 3115 is an exempt-organization application
   - C. Hybrid methods never exist
   - D. Method change is unrelated to Part 2

4. Which phrase should raise a `3115` signal?
   - A. Change in accounting method
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Gift splitting
   - D. FBAR

5. Which question should be asked early?
   - A. Is this a method issue or a period issue?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this EITC?
   - D. Is this FBAR?

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Improper method vs estimate adjustment can matter
   - B. Cash and accrual are irrelevant to business income
   - C. Method change never affects reporting
   - D. 3115 belongs to Part 1 education credits
