## Day 2 핵심

- partnership은 보통 `pass-through`
- return은 `Form 1065`
- owner 쪽에는 `K-1`
- `guaranteed payment`와 `distributive share`는 다르다
- property contribution 문제는 `basis + liabilities`를 같이 본다
- partnership 문제에 `SE income`이 붙을 수 있다

## 오늘 목표

- partnership을 `flow-through entity`로 정확히 이해한다
- `Form 1065 -> K-1 -> partner return` 흐름을 잡는다
- `guaranteed payment`, `distributive share`, `basis`, `liability`를 구분한다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- partnership인가
- entity가 세금을 내는가, partner가 내는가
- K-1 item인가
- separately stated item인가
- basis에 영향이 있나
- liability assumption이 붙나
- self-employment income 이슈가 붙나

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- `1065 -> K-1 -> partner`
- partnership income은 보통 partner 레벨로 흘러간다
- guaranteed payment는 distributive share와 같은 것이 아니다
- contribution of property 문제는 liabilities가 basis 함정이 된다
- general vs limited partner 문맥이 붙을 수 있다
- partnership-level audit and opt-out은 절차 키워드로 인식한다

## Day 2 미니퀴즈

답안은 `1C 2B ...` 형식으로 정리해서 스스로 채점하거나 튜터와 검토한다.

### 문제

1. Which form is generally filed by a partnership?
   - A. Form 1120
   - B. Form 1120S
   - C. Form 1065
   - D. Schedule C

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. A guaranteed payment is the same as a distributive share
   - B. A guaranteed payment is distinct from a distributive share
   - C. A partnership never issues K-1s
   - D. Partnership income is always taxed at the entity level

3. In a partnership contribution problem, what often needs special attention?
   - A. Filing status
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Liabilities attached to contributed property
   - D. Standard deduction

4. A partner’s share of partnership items is commonly reported to the partner on:
   - A. Form W-2
   - B. Schedule K-1
   - C. Form 1098
   - D. Form 2553

5. Which is the best default mental model for partnership taxation?
   - A. Entity-level tax only
   - B. Pass-through taxation
   - C. Gift tax regime
   - D. Payroll withholding regime

6. Which topic can show up together with partnership income questions?
   - A. Foreign tax credit only
   - B. Self-employment income
   - C. Filing status for dependents
   - D. FBAR only
