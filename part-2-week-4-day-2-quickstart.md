## 오늘 목표

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- exempt status qualification 문제인가
- status application 문제인가
- annual filing requirement 문제인가
- UBTI가 붙는가
- exempt라는 말이 filing 없음으로 잘못 읽히고 있지 않은가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- exempt status와 no filing requirement는 같은 뜻이 아니다
- `1023 / 1024`는 status application 쪽
- `990 series`는 annual filing 쪽
- UBTI는 예외적으로 과세 포인트가 될 수 있다

## Day 2 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form family is most associated with annual exempt-organization filings?
   - A. Form 990 series
   - B. Form 4797
   - C. Form 2553
   - D. Form 1041

2. Which forms are most associated with applying for exempt status?
   - A. Form 1023 / Form 1024
   - B. Form 1065 / 1120
   - C. Form 4562 / 4797
   - D. Form 709 / 706

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Tax-exempt status does not automatically mean no filing obligations
   - B. UBTI is irrelevant for exempt organizations
   - C. 990 series is an S election form
   - D. 1023 is a depreciation form

4. Which issue can create taxable consequences for an exempt organization?
   - A. UBTI
   - B. HOH
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. Schedule A only

5. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Is this qualification, filing, or UBTI?
   - B. Is this MFJ?
   - C. Is this FBAR?
   - D. Is this K-1 basis?

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Exempt organization questions often separate qualification, status maintenance, and filing
   - B. Exempt organizations never file anything
   - C. UBTI can be ignored
   - D. 1024 is a trust return
