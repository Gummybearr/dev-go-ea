## 오늘 목표

- `RAR`, `30-day letter`, `CP2000`를 같은 것으로 취급하지 않는다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- audit or correspondence issue인가
- IRS가 제안한 변경인지, 정식 appeal gate인지
- 지금 단계에서 가능한 response가 무엇인가
- substantiation / privilege / burden issue가 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- exam 문제는 `facts`, `documents`, `timing`, `appeal rights`가 같이 나온다
- `CP2000`와 일반 exam process 문서를 바로 구분해야 한다
- privilege questions are limited and fact-specific

## Day 3 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which publication is a core source for examinations and appeal rights?
   - A. Publication 556
   - B. Publication 594
   - C. Publication 1345
   - D. Publication 947

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. CP2000 and a full audit process should not be assumed to be identical
   - B. RAR means installment agreement
   - C. Substantiation never matters
   - D. Appeals rights begin only after payment

3. Which issue belongs naturally in exam questions?
   - A. substantiation
   - B. EFIN
   - C. Form 8948
   - D. ERO advertising

4. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. What stage of the exam process is this?
   - B. Is this an S election?
   - C. Is this a gift exclusion?
   - D. Is this SE tax?

5. Which document is often associated with proposed changes in an examination context?
   - A. RAR
   - B. Form 2553
   - C. Form 4562
   - D. Form 8821 only

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Audit questions often turn on procedure, not just substantive tax law
   - B. Procedure is usually irrelevant
   - C. Notices all mean the same thing
   - D. Documentation is low value
