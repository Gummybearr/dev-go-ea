## Day 3 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which pair should be distinguished in retirement-plan questions?
   - A. SEP and SIMPLE
   - B. HOH and MFJ
   - C. FBAR and 8938
   - D. AOTC and LLC

2. Which issue is a classic warning signal?
   - A. Prohibited transaction
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. Section 351
   - D. E&P

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Qualified and non-qualified plans should not be collapsed into one concept
   - B. SEP and SIMPLE are identical
   - C. Reporting requirements never matter
   - D. Retirement plans are unrelated to Part 2 specialized topics

4. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. What kind of plan is this?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this Form 4797?
   - D. Is this K-1 basis?

5. Which issue may be tested alongside plan type?
   - A. Non-discrimination rules
   - B. Filing status
   - C. FBAR
   - D. Gift splitting

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Retirement-plan questions can combine contributions, reporting, and prohibited-transaction issues
   - B. Only payroll matters here
   - C. SEP and SIMPLE can be treated as the same shortcut
   - D. Qualified status is irrelevant
