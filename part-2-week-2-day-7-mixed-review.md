1. Which distinction is foundational in Week 2?
   - A. Gross receipts vs COGS vs ordinary deductions
   - B. HOH vs MFJ
   - C. FBAR vs 8938
   - D. DNI vs corpus

2. Which form is most tied to the depreciation cluster?
   - A. Form 4562
   - B. Form 1041
   - C. Form 1024
   - D. Form 2553

3. Which form is most tied to sale of business property?
   - A. Form 4797
   - B. Form 8863
   - C. Form 709
   - D. Form 8379

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. QBI should be distinguished from ordinary deductions
   - B. QBI is just the same as all business expenses
   - C. Employment tax is the same thing as itemized deductions
   - D. 1099 reporting is unrelated to compliance

5. Which topic is best treated as a separate compliance layer?
   - A. Employment tax
   - B. Standard deduction
   - C. Child tax credit
   - D. FBAR for individuals

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. 4562 and 4797 should not be mixed up
   - B. COGS is the same thing as every ordinary deduction
   - C. Records do not matter in business compliance
