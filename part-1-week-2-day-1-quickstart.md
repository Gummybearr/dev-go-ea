1. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Adjustments happen after taxable income
   - B. Adjustments are reflected before AGI is finalized
   - C. Adjustments are the same as itemized deductions
   - D. AGI is irrelevant

2. Which schedule is a high-value trigger for many adjustments?
   - A. Schedule 1
   - B. Schedule D
   - C. Schedule SE only
   - D. Schedule E only

3. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Does this item affect AGI directly?
   - B. Is this OIC?
   - C. Is this a CDP request?

4. Which item is commonly treated as an adjustment topic?
   - A. Student loan interest
   - B. Estate tax
   - C. EFIN
   - D. Levy release

   - A. AGI matters because later deductions or credits may depend on it
   - B. AGI is just a cosmetic subtotal
   - C. Schedule 1 is low value
   - D. Above-the-line and below-the-line can be merged

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Week 2 Day 1 is about flow control inside Form 1040
   - B. Week 2 Day 1 is mostly about business entities
   - C. Week 2 Day 1 is mostly about appeals
