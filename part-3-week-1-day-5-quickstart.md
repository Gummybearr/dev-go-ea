## Day 5 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is strongly associated with due diligence for certain credits?
   - A. Form 8867
   - B. Form 1041
   - C. Form 4797
   - D. Form 1023

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Due-diligence rules can require keeping records of how, when, and from whom information was obtained
   - B. Record retention is irrelevant
   - C. Form 8867 belongs only to Part 2 business entities
   - D. PTIN never matters

3. Which question should you ask early?
   - A. Is there a due-diligence recordkeeping issue here?
   - B. Is this HOH?
   - C. Is this 3115?
   - D. Is this 4835?

4. Which cluster belongs together?
   - A. 8867 / records / signature / PTIN
   - B. 4797 / 3115 / 4562
   - C. 1041 / 990 / 4835
   - D. 1065 / 1120 / 1120S

5. Which statement is best?
   - A. Preparer obligations can overlap with ethics but still have their own procedural requirements
   - B. Preparer obligations are only about calculations
   - C. 8867 is an authorization form
   - D. Due diligence never involves documentation

6. Which issue may appear together with Form 8867 questions?
   - A. Retaining documents and worksheets
   - B. E&P
   - C. Basis
   - D. QBI
