- `Form 4797` -> sale of business property
- `Form W-2` -> employee wage / withholding signal
- `Form 1099 series` -> nonemployee pay / information reporting / compliance signal

## Day 6 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Which form is most associated with the depreciation cluster?
   - A. Form 4562
   - B. Form 1041
   - C. Form 2553
   - D. Form 1023

2. Which form is most associated with the sale of business property?
   - A. Form 4797
   - B. Form 8863
   - C. Form 709
   - D. Form 8379

3. Which form is a strong signal for employee wage reporting?
   - A. Form W-2
   - B. Form 1065
   - C. Form 1041
   - D. Form 990

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Form 1099 series may signal business compliance issues
   - B. Form 4562 and 4797 are interchangeable
   - C. Schedule C is unrelated to business income
   - D. W-2 is mainly a trust-tax form

5. Which distinction matters in Week 2 integration?
   - A. Gross receipts / COGS / deduction
   - B. HOH / MFS
   - C. FBAR / 8938
   - D. 1041 / 990

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. QBI should be kept distinct from ordinary deductions
   - B. QBI and every ordinary deduction are the same concept
   - C. 1099 reporting has nothing to do with business tax prep
   - D. Form 4797 is the main depreciation-election form
