## 오늘 목표

- business compliance 이슈를 tax prep과 연결한다
- employment tax, deposits, information reporting를 같이 본다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- employee인가 independent contractor인가
- payroll / employment tax 이슈가 붙는가
- deposit obligation이 있는가
- 1099 reporting issue인가
- record-keeping requirement가 핵심인가
- advising issue까지 이어지는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- worker classification은 tax rate 문제가 아니라 compliance 문제이기도 하다
- Form 1099 series는 business prep와 advising 양쪽에서 나온다
- accountable plan은 records와 같이 본다
- employment tax는 income tax와 별도 레이어다

## Day 5 미니퀴즈

### 문제

1. Worker classification is important partly because it affects:
   - A. Employment tax compliance
   - B. HOH only
   - C. FBAR only
   - D. Gift tax only

2. Which pair belongs together conceptually?
   - A. Accountable plans and records
   - B. E&P and HOH
   - C. DNI and Schedule A
   - D. FBAR and 2553

3. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Form 1099 reporting is unrelated to business tax prep
   - B. Employment taxes should be viewed separately from income tax
   - C. Deposits never matter
   - D. Worker classification only changes filing status

4. Which form family is a business compliance signal?
   - A. Form 1099 series
   - B. Form 8863
   - C. Form 8938 only
   - D. Form 709

5. Which issue may be tested with business reporting obligations?
   - A. Form 8300
   - B. Child tax credit
   - C. HOH
   - D. Estate deduction

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Compliance issues can overlap with advising issues
   - B. Employment taxes are the same as itemized deductions
   - C. Records never matter in vehicle or reimbursement questions
   - D. Independent contractor vs employee is irrelevant

### 정답

`1A 2A 3B 4A 5A 6A`
