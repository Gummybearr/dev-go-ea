   - A. Who claims the child?
   - B. Is this Form 706?
   - C. Is this Schedule D?
   - D. Is this a levy?

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. EITC and child care credit are the same
   - B. CTC and ODC should never be distinguished
   - C. Child-related credits can use different rules and triggers
   - D. Refundable status never matters

3. Which issue is especially associated with EITC?
   - A. Earned income
   - B. Corporate E&P
   - C. CAF
   - D. Installment sale

4. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. Refundable and nonrefundable credits should be distinguished
   - B. Refundability is irrelevant
   - C. All credits work like deductions
   - D. Credits never depend on who is claimed

5. Which form can be tied to child and dependent care credit?
   - A. Form 2441
   - B. Form 1040-X
   - C. Form 656
   - D. Form 2848

6. Which statement is best?
   - A. Week 2 Day 3 is a classification drill across several similar-looking credits
   - B. Week 2 Day 3 is mainly about business depreciation
   - C. Week 2 Day 3 is mainly about estate tax
