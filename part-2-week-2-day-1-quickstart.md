## 오늘 목표

- gross receipts와 other income을 구분한다
- COGS와 ordinary deduction을 구분한다
- accrual vs cash를 business income 문맥과 연결한다
- inventory와 UNICAP을 시험 언어 수준으로 잡는다

## 오늘 문제 읽는 순서

- cash taxpayer인가 accrual taxpayer인가
- gross receipts인가 gain인가
- COGS에 들어가는가 ordinary deduction인가
- inventory item인가
- UNICAP을 의식해야 하는가
- COD 또는 at-risk limitation 키워드가 붙는가

## 오늘 꼭 외울 것

- accrual이면 `earned but uncollected` income이 잡힐 수 있다
- `COGS`와 ordinary deduction은 다른 레이어다
- inventory 문제면 `UNICAP` 경고등
- gross receipts와 asset sale gain을 같은 것으로 보면 안 된다

### 문제

1. For an accrual-basis business, income may be included when it is:
   - A. Collected only
   - B. Earned even if not yet collected
   - C. Deposited into a retirement account
   - D. Excluded by default

2. Which statement is generally correct?
   - A. COGS and ordinary business deductions are the same line item conceptually
   - B. Inventory issues never affect COGS
   - C. Gross receipts and asset sale gain are not always the same thing
   - D. UNICAP only matters to individual filing status

3. Which topic should often come to mind in an inventory question?
   - A. UNICAP
   - B. HOH
   - C. FBAR
   - D. EITC

4. Which question should you ask early in a business income problem?
   - A. Cash or accrual?
   - B. MFJ or MFS?
   - C. AOTC or LLC?
   - D. FBAR or 8938?

5. Which is the better default distinction?
   - A. COGS vs ordinary deduction
   - B. COGS vs child tax credit
   - C. K-1 vs HOH
   - D. E&P vs FBAR

6. Which keyword may appear with business income questions and should raise a separate issue?
   - A. COD
   - B. HOH
   - C. dependent
   - D. education credit
